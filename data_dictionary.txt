BUSINESS RULES(BR) AND CONSTRAINTS

1. Customer Management

BR1 - Orders:                A single customer may place multiple orders.
BR2 - Contact Information:   Each customer is restricted to one primary contact phone number.
BR3 - Addresses:             A customer may maintain multiple shipping addresses and multiple billing addresses.
BR4 - Reviews:               A customer may submit atmost one review per product.

2. Order Management

BR5 - Contents:             An order may contain multiple products (managed via an Order Item relationship).
BR6 - Shipping:             Each order is associated with exactly one shipping address.
BR7 - Payment:              Each order is associated with exactly one payment transaction.
BR8 - Fulfillment:          Each order is fulfilled via exactly one shipment.

3. Product & Inventory

BR9 - Sourcing:             A product may be supplied by multiple suppliers.
BR10 - Categorization:       A product may belong to multiple product categories.
BR11 - Storage:              A product may be stocked in multiple warehouses.

4. Logistics & Fulfillment

BR12 - Shipments:           A shipment is linked to exactly one order and assigned to exactly one courier.
BR13 - Couriers:            A courier may be assigned multiple shipments.

5. Financials

BR14 - Payments:            A payment transaction is valid for exactly one order.
BR15 - Billing:             A single billing address may be used for multiple payment transactions.


DERIVATIONS
6. System Calculations
BR16 - Order Total:         The Total Price of an order is not a static value; it is dynamically calculated by summing the prices of all individual products within the Order Item relationship table.
